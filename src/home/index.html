About Me

Hi, I’m Keegan Churchill—a computer scientist based in Mount Pearl, Newfoundland, with a passion for designing smart and efficient systems. My journey into programming began in high school, where I was amazed by how a few well-written instructions could bring computers to life. My love for video games and problem-solving led me to merge these worlds, culminating in one of my proudest achievements: developing a StarCraft: Brood War bot using BWAPI. Watching projects incrementally improve, iterating toward efficiency and success, is something I find deeply satisfying. Whether it’s AI, game development, or solving real-world problems, I thrive on turning ideas into impactful solutions.
